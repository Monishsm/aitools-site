## Can I Pay for AI Tools from India?

Yes! Here's how:

- **Visa/Mastercard debit card** — works for almost all tools
- **PayPal** — link your Indian bank account
- **UPI** — Canva Pro accepts UPI directly
- **International credit card** — works everywhere

Most tools charge in USD. Your bank converts at current exchange rate 
(currently ~₹83-84 per dollar).
