Which is the best free AI tool in 2026?
Claude AI and ChatGPT both offer excellent free plans with no credit card required. Claude is better for writing and research. ChatGPT is better for coding and image generation. Try both for a week and see which suits you.
Which AI tool is most popular in India?
ChatGPT leads with 180 million monthly users globally. In India specifically, ChatGPT and Google Gemini are most popular due to free access and integration with Google tools. Claude AI is gaining fast among content creators and students.
Can I use AI tools to make money?
Yes. The most common ways are: affiliate marketing (earn commission recommending tools), freelancing (charge clients to create AI content), building a niche website, or selling AI-generated digital products. This website itself is an example of earning through AI tool affiliate marketing.
Is ChatGPT better than Claude AI?
They're different. Claude wins for writing quality, accuracy and long documents. ChatGPT wins for coding, image generation and plugin integrations. For most everyday users, both free plans are excellent. If you write a lot of content, Claude is better. If you code or need images, ChatGPT is better.
What is the best AI video generator for free?
InVideo AI is the best free AI video generator in 2026. It gives 10 free videos per week, no watermarks on free plan, and the quality is genuinely good. Just type a prompt and it creates a complete video with voiceover, visuals and music automatically.
